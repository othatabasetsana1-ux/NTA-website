Welcome to Our House

Ngami Theatre Activists (N.T.A) is a group that focuses on community empowerment through creativity of a theatre and its way of expressing culture. N.T.A is based on the Ngamiland region (Maun) of Botswana. The theatre brings talented people all over the country to use art as a tool for development and social awareness. The motto for the theatre is “Capitalizing Creativity for Education with Empowerment by Entertainment”. The N.T.A was founded in 2007 by a poet, a singer, an actor and a comedian who goes by the name Boitshoko Smallz Samotebe. He created it with an intention which is to share skills, to nurture art and make use of art
