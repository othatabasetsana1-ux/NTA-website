NTA's Story

Ngami Theatre Activists is a theatre and playmaking collaborative of youth in Botswana,basically in the Ngamiland region. It was established in Maun and it originally absorbed only youth in high alcohol and crime locations in Maun such as Sanyedi, Sedie, Thito and others. The objective being to use theatre and playmaking as methods of rehabilitating these participants and providing them with alternative sober reaction. In 2007 the group began attracting the interests and attention of the youth and professionals accross the country who wanted to ply their trade with it. It was then transformed into a full time theatre group and began participating in flagship programmes of the Ministry of Youth Empowerment, Sports and Culture Development. Ngami Theatre Activists now has a national reach and serves youth at national level.Owing to the mixed skills and nature, the group is structured in two ways;

NGAMI THEATRE ACTIVISTS MAIN GROUP

This is comprised of 15 youth of different backgrounds, rehabilitated offenders, alcohol abusers and general artists. It participates in the Ministry of Sports and Arts actvities through the National Arts Coucil of Botswana and other national activities. It provides community support services to individuals, NGOs and Govermental departments contributing positively to communities in Ngamiland and accross Botswana. In order to continue its good works, the group survives through community and stakeholders' donations and help.

NGAMI THEATRE ACTIVISTS TRAVELLING PARTY

The travelling party is a mobile part of the group comprised of five highly professionals and specialized theatre activists and faccilitators. It conducts commercial work that support the general vission of Ngami Theatre Activists and performs in high compliance national and international theatre events, festivals etc.
